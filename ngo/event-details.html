Feast of Unity:

The “Community Feast” is presented by our family in the spirit of unity, love, and common humanity named “Harmony Table”.This is more than just a lunch. It's an immersive event where people come together in a community to share the nuances of food and life and forge bonds that transcend the boundaries of context. Breaking bread together as a community becomes a symbolic act that transcends socio-economic and cultural boundaries. Eating together acts as a powerful bonding medium, promoting a sense of camaraderie and togetherness among participants.
